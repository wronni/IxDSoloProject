## OpenSports

### Local Sports Event Organization Platform

**Website:** https://www.opensports.net

**Company Brief Description:**

OpenSports is a platform that organizes local sports games and events with a focus on logistics and event management. The service handles the operational aspects of running sports activities, including payment processing, RSVP management, venue coordination, and participant tracking. OpenSports aims to make it easier for organizers to run sports events by streamlining administrative tasks, collecting fees, and managing attendance. The platform is particularly strong in handling the business side of sports events.

---

## Pros

- **Handles payments well** - Integrated payment processing for game fees
- **RSVP management** - Reliable attendance tracking and confirmations
- **Event organization** - Strong tools for managing sports events
- **Venue coordination** - Location and facility management features
- **Financial tracking** - Payment history and fee management
- **Automated reminders** - Participant notifications and updates
- **Capacity management** - Player limits and waitlists
- **Professional interface** - Clean, business-oriented design

---

## Cons

- **Focused on logistics, not gameplay** - Prioritizes operations over game quality
- **No team balancing** - Does not address fair team formation
- **No fairness features** - Lacks tools to ensure competitive balance
- **Administrative focus** - Designed for organizers, not players
- **Missing AI intelligence** - No smart matching or balancing algorithms
- **Limited player experience** - Minimal features for improving actual gameplay
- **No skill-level matching** - Players are not matched by ability
- **Organizer-centric** - Player needs secondary to administrative functions
- **Doesn't solve uneven teams** - Games can still be lopsided and unfair
- **No competitive balance tools** - Team formation is manual and arbitrary
