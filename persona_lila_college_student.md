# Persona: Lila - The College Student

**IXD    GROUP4    FAIRPLAY - SMART TEAM ORGANIZER**

---

## Overview

Lila represents the young, socially-motivated player who uses sports as a way to meet new people and stay active. She faces challenges finding games at her skill level and often feels excluded from established groups. She needs an inclusive platform that helps her find welcoming sports experiences that fit her busy student schedule.

---

## Persona Profile

**Name:** Lila

**Age:** 19

**Status:** College Student

**Type:** The College Student

---

## Goals

- Join pickup basketball games to meet people and stay active
- Find inclusive sports communities
- Participate in games that fit her class schedule
- Build social connections through sports

---

## Frustrations

- Hard to find games at her skill level
- Often feels left out of teams
- Established groups can be intimidating or unwelcoming
- Limited time due to academic commitments
- Uncertainty about whether she'll be welcomed at pickup games

---

## Motivation

Looking for inclusive, social sports experiences that fit her schedule. Lila is motivated by the social aspect of sports and wants to find communities where she feels welcomed regardless of her skill level.

---

## User Needs

- Skill-level filtering to find appropriate games
- Welcoming, inclusive game environments
- Flexible scheduling that works with student life
- Social features to connect with other players
- Low barrier to entry for new participants
- Clear communication about game expectations

---

## Design Opportunities

- Implement skill-level indicators and matching
- Create beginner-friendly game categories
- Build social profiles and connection features
- Provide schedule flexibility and easy booking
- Include inclusivity ratings or tags for games
- Enable pre-game communication to reduce anxiety
- Show player diversity and welcoming indicators
